About Me

Hey, I'm ijs! an 18 year old full-stack programmer based in the Netherlands. I've been a Roblox developer for 6 years, I always go above and beyond to deliver highly efficient and modular scripts with as little vulnerabilities as possible.

TERMS OF SERVICE

If you hire me you agree to:

  • You must pay a % of the payment upfront.
  • You have a 3 day period where you can refund the upfront payment if you don't like the progress or systems.
  • After the 3 day period I will assume you like the progress and the upfront payment can NOT be refunded anymore.
  • I choose my own hours in which I will work. Do not treat me like your employee.
  • I'm allowed to stop the commission and refund you the payment if I don't want to continue anymore. Even if the 3 day period passed, I will still refund you the full % you have paid.
